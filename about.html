About Us

We Serve Fresh & Healthy Coffee

Founded in 2015 by passionate coffee enthusiasts, Coffee House began as a small dream in the heart of Pune. What started as a single location has grown into a beloved community hub, driven by our unwavering commitment to quality, sustainability, and customer satisfaction. We source our beans from ethical farms around the world, ensuring fair trade practices and environmental responsibility.

Our Story

It all began in 2015 when a group of friends, united by their passion for exceptional coffee, decided to turn their shared dream into reality. Starting with a modest setup in Pune. We focused on sourcing the finest beans from sustainable farms and creating a space where people could gather, relax, and enjoy. Over the years, we've expanded our reach, but our core mission remains the same: to provide an unparalleled coffee experience that fosters community and well-being. From our first cup to thousands served daily,every milestone has been a testament to our dedication to quality and innovation.

Our Commitment to Sustainability

At Coffee House, sustainability is at the heart of everything we do. We partner with certified fair-trade farms to ensure our beans are grown responsibly, minimizing environmental impact. From biodegradable packaging to energy-efficient equipment, we're dedicated to reducing our carbon footprint and supporting eco-friendly practices. Join us in making a positive difference, one cup at a time.
